Welcome to the head injury study

Athletics are an important part of the college career of many Division III students. However, participation in athletics can sometimes result in injury, including head injury or concussion. While there has been a lot of attention to the long term effects of multiple concussions in professional athletes, it is unclear if there are any long term risks of concussion in children or young adult adults. This study is designed to address this important question. We are grateful for your time and participation.
